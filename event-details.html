TechFest 2024: Innovation Summit

December 15, 2024 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Main Auditorium 1,234 Registered

About This Event

TechFest 2024 is the premier technology festival bringing together students, professionals, and innovators for a day of learning, competition, and networking. This year's theme focuses on "Innovation for Tomorrow" with cutting-edge workshops, hackathons, and tech talks from industry leaders.

Join us for an exciting day filled with hands-on workshops, competitive programming challenges, and inspiring keynote sessions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, there's something for everyone at TechFest 2024.
